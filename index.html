About Me

I am Hamed, a recent graduate with a Master's degree in Industrial Engineering from Kharazmi University, where I defended my thesis on modeling a bi-objective closed-loop supply chain with robust optimization, considering imperfect quality and on-time delivery, under the supervision of Dr. Hamid Reza Pasandideh.

My main research interests are operations research, mathematical modeling, supply chain management, and machine learning. I am actively seeking Ph.D. opportunities in these fields to contribute to advancing knowledge in these domains.

Additionally, I am strongly interested in quality control, inventory management, healthcare systems, and data mining. I am eager to connect with researchers and professors worldwide.
